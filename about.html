Heritage & story

The culinary history behind GustoOro’s amber dining vision.

In the heart of the city, GustoOro was conceived as a modern luxury restaurant blending Michelin sensibilities with a warm, luminous atmosphere. Our story is rooted in chef-led hospitality, bespoke tasting menus, and an unwavering focus on refined ingredients.

Philosophy

Our kitchen is a studio where every plate is composed with a golden ratio of flavor, texture, and visual drama, designed for guests who value elevated service and memorable ambiance.

Michelin legacy

A curated team with global accolades.

Since opening, GustoOro has been shaped by chefs with Michelin backgrounds and boutique culinary houses. We tailor every service around locally sourced produce, custom cocktails, and an artful wine program.
